Child Labor Topic












Why

The sweat beads run down my forehead

Like the rain from a cold winter’s night

The shine in my eyes that my mother once loved

Were now swept over by clouds of grey



I walk to the windowpane and lean over

I watch the children play and a tear runs down my face

I wipe the tear then I wonder

Why me?



I’ve had everything taken from me

I don’t know my age

I don’t know my voice

I don’t know even know myself

Again I wonder

Why me?



Why did I have to be the victim of it all?

I, a small child, was working 14 hours day

Getting paid 40 cents per day

And still, I don’t know

Why

Social Media and Relationships Topic

For the social media project, students chose a subject that social media has has impacted and wrote not only a poem but made a multimedia collage.






Unrequited

At the sight of you, I smile

As if you’re my sun and I’m the Earth

Because my world does revolve around you

I take pictures of you to savor them

Because pictures are timeless

I post them online for the whole world to see

Because you’re someone I’m proud of

When you go away, I miss you

But seeing how you are,

What you’re doing,

And how much fun you’re having

Makes me feel more connected to you

At 10:25 PM my whole world broke

I saw the post of you and your “friend”

And from then I knew it had to end

I realized that even though you were my world,

I was never yours

All the pictures

All the trips

All the hugs

All the “i love you’s”

Were all a lie
